~> Hobbies & Interests

COOKING

Talking about hobbies and interests, Let's talk about Cooking. I started taking interest in cooking since I was 12 and if you're wondering I am 17. I liked looking at cooking tutorials and reading cook books by famous cooks like Joshua Weissman and Nick D'Giovanni.

Nowadays, I cook almost every day and try new recipes every weekend. Cooking is like free therapy to me, this helps me cool down and be stress-free as well . Cooking is a natural medicine to me cause, while I cook I get into a zen like state and it makes me happy when my friends and family get exiced to taste my cooking.

TRAVELLING

Let's be honest, we've all seen Man vs Wild in T.V. and thought "I want to do crazy shit like he does, I want to travel around the world to do these things", and so have I. Watching that crazy dude so some crazy stuff always got me excited, so I gravitated towards it.

I go to places I've never been before, go trekking, hiking and many more with friends and families. I like going to explore nature and it's strange creation, away from the society filled with tech and pollution. Travelling is a means to escape the technologies to me.

MUSIC

Music has been a part of my life from since I can remember. A little backstory, I have an elder sibling and he would always play music on the living room tv. If you can remember there used to be a music streaming channel called VH1 studios and my brother used to play music on it daily. If you are wondering What genre of music I listen to, it's Indie and Jazz.

I got into music, thanks to my brother. Later on he taught me how to play the Guitar, Ukulele, Piano and many more. I am still expanding my knowledge in music everyday and will continue this forever.
